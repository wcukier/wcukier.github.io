I'm an Astronomy and Astrophysics PhD Student at UChicago working with Diana Powell.
I model clouds on exoplanets microphysically — taking into account interactions on a
molecular scale. I particularly work on understanding the role clouds play in brown dwarf
demographic trends such as the L–T transition and low gravity sequence.

I additionally work on making the powerful software tools I use available to other
researchers. To this end I am developing
[CARMApy](https://carmapy.readthedocs.io), an open source Python wrapper of
the CARMA cloud code for the exoplanet community. Reach out if you'd like to beta test!
